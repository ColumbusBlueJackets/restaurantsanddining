Take a Bite out of Columbus

You'll never go hungry or thirsty in this city. Columbus boasts some of the best culinary experiences in America. Not to mention some of the top beverage spots around!

Quick Eats

Need a quick bite to eat after practice? North Market and Mikey's Late Night Slice are two iconic establishments and are just a short walk from Nationwide Arena!


Premiere Dining

Special occasions call for special dinners. Pop on over to one of Cameron Mitchell's signature restaurants, including Martini's, The Pearl, Marcella's or The Guild House!


Rooftop Bars

What's better than a cold drink with excellent views? Check out Lincoln Social or one of the many rooftop bars in Columbus for an elegant night out on the town.

Coffee Shops

If you ever need a quick pick-me-up, Columbus boasts numerous quaint coffee shops like Roosevelt's and Stauf's as well as Canada's favorite coffee shop: Tim Horton's!

Breweries

The Brewery District is home to a number of laid-back hangouts like Land Grant, Brew Dog and Taft's Brewpourium. Grab some buds and enjoy your favorite beer!

Distilleries

High Bank and Watershed are just two famous names on a long list of distilleries in Columbus. Relax with your favorite cocktail or take a walk on the Distillery Trail!

Sweets

Donuts? Chocolate? Desserts? Columbus has it all at eateries like Buckeye Donuts, Winan's and the Macaroon Bar down the street from Nationwide Arena in the Short North!
